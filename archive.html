2022 was very generous year for our team, and we’d like to take a quick look back at the projects we’ve been working on: We were part of the mentor program at the Center for Social Vision, newly created project of the contemporary art platform Swimming pool. Within 6 months we discussed and debated together with the participants their projects and tried to create a field for exchange of experiences, independent policies and action about the places and things we all share. As a completion of our work at the Center for Social Vision, we participated at the public program “Negotiation” taking part in the discussions “How we think and do the social” and “A-common-vision-for-the-city”. We also realized a lecture with a discussion about our vision for design education in the presentation “Together, To gather, To get there”
